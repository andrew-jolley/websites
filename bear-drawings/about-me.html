About Me!

Hi! I'm Charlotte, I'm 17 and from the UK! For most of my time I'm a busy A-level student working towards going to uni next year, but art has always been a hobby of mine which is why I chose to start Bear Drawings!
My favourite types of art, those I sell, are digital art and oil painting. I love creating art of musicians that inspire me, and I particularly enjoy painting subjects such as F1 cars, landscapes or aeroplanes.
I hope you enjoy looking through my gallery and maybe even find something you'd like to own! Thank you so much for taking the time to support me :)
